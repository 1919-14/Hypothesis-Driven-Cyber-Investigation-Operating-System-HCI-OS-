## 🚀 What is HCI-OS?

HCI-OS (Hypothesis-Driven Cyber Investigation Operating System) is a cyber resilience platform engineered specifically to secure India's critical sectors (including AIIMS, CBSE, and Power Grids).

Unlike traditional Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) systems that generate thousands of isolated alerts for manual triage, HCI-OS operates on a proactive **Hypothesis-Driven** model. It continuously ingests normalized telemetry, matches behaviors against known threat structures, generates competing Bayesian hypotheses about attacker activity, validates decisions via an adversarial Critic model, and secures responses with an immutable, cryptographically chained audit log.

### Key Business Metrics
- **Systemic ROI:** 20,000x return on investment.
- **Risk-Adjusted ROI:** 1,000x (adjusted for a conservative 5% annual incident probability).
- **Break-Even Point:** Preventing a single major AIIMS-style outage covers operating costs for 200 years.
- **Compliance SLA:** Auto-generates CERT-In-compliant reports in seconds, closing the regulatory 6-hour reporting gap.
